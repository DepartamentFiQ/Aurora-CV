Aula d'informàtica mòbil

Faced with the lack of a IT classroom at the center, we thought a mobile computer room was needed. As a solution, a charging cabinet with capacity for 24 laptops, which could be brought to the classroom when needed was purchased and in the recent past our school was fited with WIFI acces in all the classrooms.  We must thank the management team for providing us with all the necessary equipment, as well as hours of co-teaching for each group.
